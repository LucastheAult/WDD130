Bulborb

This creature is found asleep a lot of the time. If provoked, it will attack anything around it with it's large mouth.

Burrowing Snagret

This creature spends most of its time underground, but when something comes near its territory, it comes out of the ground to attack. It uses its beak to attack its prey.

Fiery Blowhog

This creature blows fire out of its long trunk. It likes to lurk in darker and dank environments. If your pikmin are set on fire, put it out by using Olimar's whistle.

Wollywog

This is a frog like creature that has impressive jumping abilities. A white varient of this creature can be found in different areas of the planet.

Beady Long Legs

This creature is one of the biggest in the game. Its big feet are capable of crushing you. Its weakest point is its giant head that hangs low

Puffy Blowhog

This creature roams around floating off the ground. When it spots a you it blow a gust of wind that will blow away your pikmin.

Swooping Snitchbug

This creature roams around flying with its wing antennas. If it spots your pikmin, it will swoop down and try to grab them. It will then replant them into the ground by throwing them down.

Emperor Bulblax

This creature is part of the same family of the bulborb. It burries itself in the ground to where only its foliage on its back and bulbous eyes are visible. If approached it will come out from the ground and attack you.
