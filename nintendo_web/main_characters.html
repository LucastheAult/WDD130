Captain Olimar

Captain Olimar is the character that you play as in the game. He is from the Planet Hocotate and has crash landed on the planet PNF-404 where the pikmin inhabit. His mission is to put his ship back together with the help of the pikmin and get back to his home planet.

The Red Pikmin

This is the first type of Pikmin that you discover in the game. They are resistant to fire so they are your best choice when it comes to fire breathing enemies. They also hit the hardest in combat.

The Yellow Pikmin

This is the second pikmin that you discover in the game. They have increased throwing height due to their larger ears. They can also pick up bomb rocks and use them to blow up walls or enemies.

The Blue Pikmin

This is the third and final type of pikmin that you discover in the game. They are able breath underwater due to them having gills.
